Uses a Raspberry Pi with a Retropie installation on the micro SD card. A great way to to "emulate" older systems and play ROMS.

### Parts
* [Raspberry Pi 3 Model B+](https://www.raspberrypi.org/products/raspberry-pi-3-model-b-plus/)
* [DUALSHOCK 4 wireless controller](https://www.playstation.com/en-gb/explore/accessories/dualshock-4-wireless-controller/)
* [Samsung EVO Plus 32GB microSDHC](https://www.samsung.com/uk/memory-storage/evo-plus-microsd-card-with-sd-adapter/MB-MC32DAEU/)
